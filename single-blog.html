2019 Customer Survey Winners!!

  • Lifestyle
  • 03 Comments

One of the most important parts of the 8x8 culture is we are customer focused. We want to provide the best possible service and build the right products for our customers. Each year we send out a survey to our customers and award a few lucky winners. This year we picked ten lucky customers and each won a $200 Amazon gift card - a small token of our appreciation. Here are the lucky winners and a little more about the companies they work for.

Customer Survey Winners

Steve from New Life Resources
An 8x8 customer since 2013, New Life Resources is a mental health counseling practice in Wisconsin.

Thomas from Thomas Weiss and Associates, P.C.
A customer since 2016, Thomas Weiss and Associates, P.C. is a law firm in Long Island, New York.

George from Gulf Coast Fire & Integration
A customer since 2018, Gulf Coast Fire & Integration specializes in alarm installations in Florida.

Laurene from Satellite Data Inc
A customer since 2015, Satellite Data provides payroll services in Ohio.

Jon from eVenture Zone
A customer since 2011, eVenture Zone provides hospitality services for the Paralympic Games.

Hugh from Port Networks
A customer since 2009, Port Networks is a local internet service provider in Maryland.

Richard from Red and White Fleet
A customer since 2008, Red and White Fleet provides cruises in the San Francisco Bay.

Garen from Hilltop Dental
A customer since 2018, Hilltop Dental is a dental clinic in California.

Jason from Okogen
A customer since 2018, Okogen is a pharmaceutical company specializing in eye diseases.

Maria from 1412 Management LLC
A customer since 2017, 1412 Management LLC is a property management company.

Thank you again to everyone who participated, congratulations to the gift card winners and continue to provide your feedback!
