What is VoIP all about?

VoIP is a methodology for the delivery of voice communications and multimedia sessions, such as video calls, over internet protocol networks, such as the internet.

Advantages of VoIP over traditional contact methods:

  • Easier to install, configure and maintain
  • Old technologies, like fax, is supported
  • Lower rate than a traditional phone call
  • Hosted VoIP saves businesses money
  • VoIP integrates well with other business systems, such as outlook
